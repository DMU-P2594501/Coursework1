    • Gary Oldman:

    Age: 21st March 1958 (62)

    Nationality: English

    Gary Oldman is another actor out of this talented cast to have won many awards in his career for his breath-taking roles and is most famous for his part in Harry Potter, Darkest Hour, The Dark Knight Trilogy, and many more compelling films. In this film, Gary portrays a LT in the GCPD (Gotham City Police Department) where he works alongside both Batman and Harvey Dent to take down organised crime that has been contaminating Gotham's core. He undergoes plenty of challenges as he proves that he is one of Gotham's purest police officers.
